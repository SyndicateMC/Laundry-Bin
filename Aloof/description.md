### 📄**Gist:**

Aloof is a small mod focused on adding very small, inconsequential features to Minecraft in the form of new Sniffer plants!

Currently in the process of porting features from the old version of the mod, that being [this](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/aloof).

![divvy](https://github.com/SyndicateMC/Laundry-Bin/blob/main/General/Icons/divider.png?raw=true)

### 📖**Breakdown:**

This mod currently adds **Loofah**, a new crop you can obtain from seeds that the Sniffer can dig up! This new crop is planted on moss, and requires two blocks of space to grow. When fully grown, it will become the Loofah Gourd, a two-tall block that is very, brightly green and striped. This gourd can then be stripped with an axe (or any item that can strip wood) to turn it into the Loofah Sponge, which functions like a weaker sponge!
