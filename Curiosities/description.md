### 📄**Gist:**

Curiosities is a general enhancement mod focused on adding new metals, tools, building materials, equipment, quality of life, consistency, and really whatever we think fits. Adds all matter of things such as materials like that of Aluminum, Nickel, and Invar, along with additional building blocks like Fancied Planks and Ashlar!

This mod currently is divided into two parts: vanilla enhancement, and new content. "Vanilla enhancement" as in small QoL and extra fill-in-the-gaps content that strictly enhances the vanilla experience. "New content" as in specialized content from the mod that amplifies the Minecraft experience with fresh and new toys to play with.

![divvy](https://github.com/SyndicateMC/Laundry-Bin/blob/main/General/Icons/divider.png?raw=true)

### 📖**Breakdown:**

For the vanilla enhancement side, there are new building blocks like that of the Fancied Planks and Ashlar (bricks) made with vanilla woods and stones.

There are also a **TON** of changes and additions like redstone component retextures and backported models, smooth stone retextures and full blockset (i.e. Smooth Stone Bricks), backported note block noises for copper blocks, and missing stairs, walls, and slabs for certain building blocks (like prismarine and quartz).

For new content, there are new materials like Aluminum, Nickel, and Invar.

__Aluminum__ is building-centric, with a new armor and toolset centered around benefits to building -- along with a blockset that looks quite industrial and/or brutalist or even office-like.

__Nickel__ is a new conductive and heat-resistant metal, being able to be used in the making of new redstone components like the Redstone Diode and the Redstone Fuse.

__Invar__ is a new ancient alloy made with Ancient Scrap (was Netherite Scrap), Iron, and Nickel. Invar is the "immutable substance", used to make durable armor that provides **FLAT DAMAGE REDUCTION** and tools that can pierce armor and damage resistances. It can also be used to make new Invar Weights, blocks that are effected by gravity and will fall after a certain amount of time (dictated by the amount of Invar used to craft them).
